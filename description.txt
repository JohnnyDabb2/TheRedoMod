This mod currently adds 3 items and 1 boss.  There is far more content coming, so downloading it now wouldn't be a bad idea.  Join the discord server by clicking the homepage button!



CURRENT FEATURES

1) The Money Trough can be crafted with a piggy bank and 100 cloud, making it far easier to obtain.

2) Players have a new starting item in their inventory, the Goodie Bag.  Right click on this item for a pleasant surprise!

3) Princess Pinky is a new boss added into terraria via this mod. She is spawned with a Pinky Crown.



KNOWN ISSUES

1) When Princess Pinky is jumping, her sprite becomes a giant rectangle.

2) Not enough content!
